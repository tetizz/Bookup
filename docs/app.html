Scoring method

Chess.com’s CAPS2 formula and phase boundaries are not public. Bookup uses Stockfish expected-points loss: Book and engine-best moves are 100%, then each remaining move is scored by how much winning expectation it gives away. Opening, middlegame, and endgame are separated with transparent move-count and material rules.
