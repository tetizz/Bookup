Bookup for Windows

Install the local repertoire trainer.

The full Bookup experience runs on your computer: it imports your public Chess.com games, stores your repertoire locally, analyzes with bundled Stockfish, and can use your private optional Lichess token without sending it to a website.

Why desktop first

The real trainer needs local files, local cache, and a real engine.

The website can show the workflow, but the desktop release is where imports, background analysis, saved training progress, and Stockfish settings actually run.

01

Local game import

Build a move tree from your public Chess.com games and save it in your local Bookup data folder.

02

Bundled Stockfish

Analyze positions with MultiPV lines, cached evaluations, and configurable time/depth settings.

03

Private study cache

Keep lesson progress, known lines, repeated misses, and Lichess context on your own machine.
